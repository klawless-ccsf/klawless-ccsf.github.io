St. Maarten

 St. Martin is the smallest island in the world divided between two countries, France and the Netherlands. The northern French part of the island is known as St. Martin. The southern Dutch part is known as St. Maarten. St.Martin’s Princess Juliana International Airport has a ridiculously short runway that is located between a large hill and a beach. There are some wild photo opts when a plane is coming in.

Conch & Dumplings comes from the Dutch Caribbean side of the island. You will be able to get this hot and spicy dish come carnivale season. The conch is pounded and then pressure cooked with seasonings. The dumplings are prepared with flour, and with cornmeal. The thick sauce from the conch and dumplings is used as gravy.
